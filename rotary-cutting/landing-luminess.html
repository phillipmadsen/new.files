What is True-Color?

Without True-Color Balanced Light™ Notice how the fabric and thread looks dull and lifeless. Even the hand looks like it has been left in the sun too long. It's not any of these things that has a problem though, it's the lighting. While quilting has been done for many years under this lighting, new bright fabrics, such as batik, can only be truly appreciated under high quality lighting. Even standard fabrics and threads, such as shown here, will also benefit from this lighting. For perfect matching of hues and values, regular lighting just doesn't cut it.

With True-Color Balanced Light™True-Color Balanced Light™ reduces glare and soften shadows while enhancing details and textures. The 24 watt T5 florescent bright-white 6500K bulbs provide accurate colors with no tinting or false color cast. These bulbs also produce less heat than standard lighting systems, which makes you and your fabric less tired after working. The fabulous colors were already there, making the Luminess perfect for artisits and crafters. The colors spring to life so you can create beautiful, lively quilts.
